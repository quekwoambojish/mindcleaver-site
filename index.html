What is MindCleaver?

"Villages grow, factions rise, banditry finds a fresh start, and dragons begin their hunt... The Mindguard's powers will weave into the social fabrics of their being, contorting a reformed world."

MindCleaver is a book adaptation set in an old-magic fantasy world where every character thinks, feels, and acts on its own. As a wizard whose immortal essence is bound to a blade, you enter a living world, and begin to twist the minds inside it.

Shift the personalities of those around you between bravery, fear, agony, kindness, and pleasure. Watch alliances tremble, leaders fall, and communities transform based on what you have done to the minds inside them.

Within this world, you compete against other Mindguard, whose power's rival your own. Trapped in this infinite cycle of rebirth they have shed their mortal cares for the world, staunch and prideful with power they seek to make these lands under their bidding.

Should the world be bound by their dark trance, an eternity of oppression looms over you...

Behind the Story

As a tribute to my father's Mindguard book series, I wanted to pass his memory down to my son in my own way. This game is based on a ritual one of the powerful Mindguard performed from the books he wrote.

When a Mindguard became powerful enough, they could trap their essence into a blade in order to live on forever...The player, and the antagonists, are these Mindguard. Their blades scattered across the world.

MindCleaver is currently in Mid Alpha, and is in closed alpha on Steam. Stay tuned in for more updates!
